Frequently asked questions

About the app and the Georgian theory exam.

Are the questions the same as in the official exam?

Yes. DriveGE uses the official theory exam questions of the LEPL Service Agency of the Ministry of Internal Affairs of Georgia, so you practice on exactly what you will see at the exam.

Which languages does DriveGE support?

English, Russian and Georgian — both the interface and the exam questions. You can switch at any time.

Which driving licence categories are covered?

A (motorcycles), B (cars), C (trucks), D (buses) and AM (mopeds) — each with its own official question bank and exam parameters.

Is DriveGE free?

Yes, the app is free to download and start practicing. The full experience is unlocked with DriveGE Pro, with a free trial included.

How does the mock exam work?

Mock exams follow the official rules of the real Georgian theory test: the same number of questions, the same allowed number of mistakes and the same timer, so you know exactly how ready you are.

Which devices are supported?

iPhone, iPad, Mac (Apple silicon) and Apple Vision Pro.
