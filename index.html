About App Assistance

App Assistance is the home of iOS apps built by independent developer Artem Dorozhkin. The idea is simple: take one everyday task — passing a driving exam, tracking a meal, scanning a document, sending an invoice — and make it effortless. Each app is designed to be fast, private and pleasant to use, with support for iPhone, iPad and beyond.
